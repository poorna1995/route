\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We studied \textbf{routing from unsupervised signals} in a fixed model pool with distinct capabilities.
We studied \textbf{binary LLM routing} from unsupervised signals: oracle $r(q)$ for appropriate-model analysis, three information layers (H1--H3) tested alone, frozen $x(q)$, and \textbf{binary model-selection} via $s(q)=\lambda^\top x(q)$ and $\pi(q)$, comparing hand-designed and learned weights (H4) under accuracy--cost Pareto evaluation.
We evaluate with accuracy--cost Pareto curves on test, separate from signal analysis on calib.
