\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We studied \textbf{routing from unsupervised signals} in a fixed model pool with distinct capabilities.
We defined routing need $r(q)$, extracted query-derived, model-response, and cross-model comparative signals without routing supervision at extraction time, analyzed which predict $r(q)$ (H1--H3), froze $x(q)$, and routed via $s(q)=\lambda^\top x(q)$ with threshold policy $\pi(q)$, comparing hand-designed and learned weights (H4).
We evaluate with accuracy--cost Pareto curves on test, separate from signal analysis on calib.
