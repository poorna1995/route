\paragraph{Summary.}
H1--H3 answer \emph{informativeness per layer}: each block may predict $r(q)$ strongly, weakly, or not at all.
A positive H2 with a weak H1 is valid---model-dependent uncertainty may carry routing information without query-side cues.
Combination models ($\phi{+}\psi$, $\phi{+}\psi{+}\chi$) inform frozen $x(q)$ selection but are not substitutes for the layer hypotheses.
