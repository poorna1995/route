\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

Our method follows five **pipeline steps** (Figure~\ref{fig:pipeline}): signal extraction, signal analysis, signal selection, routing policy, and evaluation---corresponding to execution Stages 5--9 after the setting is locked (Stages 1--4).
Signal analysis and routing evaluation are \textbf{separate} scientific claims: H1--H3 test prediction of $r(q)$; H4 tests deployable routing quality.
